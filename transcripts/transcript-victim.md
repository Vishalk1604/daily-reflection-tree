## Why This Path Is Written This Way

The most important design constraint in the assignment is this: *"the tree must not moralize."* The Drift path is the hardest path to write without shame.

Notice what the reflections on this path do **not** say:
- They don't call the employee a victim
- They don't suggest the entitlement feeling is wrong
- They don't frame the self-orientation as selfish

What they do instead: name the experience accurately, acknowledge it as common and real, and offer exactly one small forward-looking question. The `EDGE_CASE_EXTERNAL_ENTITLE` node is especially deliberate — it names "learned helplessness" not as a diagnosis but as a risk worth watching for, and the only ask is a micro-experiment for tomorrow.

The tree treats both paths as valid endpoints. The summaries read differently — but neither reads like a verdict.
